

JavaScript interpreter which evaluates
JavaScript *expressions*.


supported:

- **expression** *e* ::= *v* | *uop* *e* | *e* *bop* *e*

- **value** *v* ::= *n* | *b*

- **unary operator** *uop* ::= `-` | `!`

- **binary operator** *bop* ::= `+` | `-` | `*` | `/` | `===` | `!==` | `<` | `<=` | `>` | `>=` | `&&` | `||`

- **number (float)** *n*

- **boolean** *b* ::= `true` | `false`
